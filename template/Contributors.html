Project Contributors

Meet the minds behind the project

Ansh Arora

Ansh gathered all the important data from a website called Kaggle. He made sure our data was good by finding and fixing any weird or wrong numbers. Ansh then taught the computer to make predictions about house prices using a method called Linear Regression. He also made it easy for people to use our predictions by building a special website part called a Flask server.

Arman Qureshi

Arman is the person who made our project look nice on the internet! He created a cool home page where you can see our predictions and play with them. Arman also created a webpage on our website to let people know about our project abd made sure it's easy for anyone to use our project on their computers. He's the one who made everything look so good and work smoothly.

Karan Singh

Karan makes sure that our data is clean and ready for the computer to understand. He looked closely at our data to find interesting things about houses and prices and extract the importance features from the data and improve the efficiency of the data. Karan also find the insight from the data using Exploratory data analysis (EDA).

Piyush Negi

Piyush made a page on our website where you can learn about the people who worked on this project. Piyush also gathered helpful information and resources and created a webpage for people who want to know more about predicting real estate prices.
